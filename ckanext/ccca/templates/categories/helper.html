Categories are assigned to each Dataset within the "Keywords" tab of the Metadata Editor. We support the following categories according to Inspire and ISO: {#} (TopicCategory) (tpCat) 19115 {#} {#} Copernicus Categories http://climate.copernicus.eu/about-c3s {#} {#} definitions found here: https://www2.usgs.gov/science/about/thesaurus-full.php?thcode=15{#}

Agriculture and Forestry:
Rearing of animals or cultivation of plants. (Definition)

Phenology:
Flora and/or fauna in natural environment. (Definition)

Meteorology/Climatology:
Processes and phenomena of the atmosphere. (Definition)

Tourism:
Economic activities, conditions and employment (Definition)

Health:
Health, health services, human ecology, and safety. (Definition)

Water Management/Hydrology:
Inland water features, drainage systems and characteristics. (Definition)

Infrastructure:
Man-made construction. (Definition)

Transport):
Means and aids for conveying persons or goods. (Definition)

Energy:
Energy, water and waste systems and communications infrastructure and services. (Definition)

Disaster/Risk Reduction:
Systematic approaches identifying, assessing and reducing the risks of disaster (Explanation). See also Copernicus
